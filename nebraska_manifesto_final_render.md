The machine is built in a specific, anti-entropic order. Inverting this order — starting with dialogue or "cool scenes" — guarantees structural failure.

- **Fuel/Corpse Components:** What must break or fail to prove the stakes are real?
- **Gravity Wells:** Non-negotiable masses (like a character who must die) that bend the narrative space around them.

### Layer 3: The Expression (The Render)
