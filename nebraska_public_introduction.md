## THE DISCOVERY: THE A/B PATTERN

Imagine you're at a lakehouse with old friends. Everyone's being polite, remembering the good times, making soup. It's nice. But it's not real. It's what we call a **B-Path** — a predetermined script everyone follows to avoid uncomfortable choices.

Now imagine someone brings a mysterious footlocker onboard. Smells weird. Could have anything inside. Suddenly, everyone has to make real choices: open it or not? Protect it or throw it overboard? Lie about it or confess?

That footlocker is what we call an **A-Path injection**. It forces the system (the friend group) to stop following a script and start making real choices. Each choice leads to the next, creating a chain of logic that becomes a real story.

---

## THE LAW: A/B = STORY

This works everywhere.

```
A (Choice) ÷ B (Constraint) = Coherent Meaning
```

| State | Condition | Output |
|-------|-----------|--------|

| Too much B | All rules, no choice | Rigidity — correct but dead |
| ~60/40 A-dominant | The sweet spot | Living, adaptive meaning |

## NEBRASKA 0.0: THE CHOICE/PATH DOCTRINE — FORMAL DEFINITION

Nebraska 0.0 is the foundational operating principle from which all other versions derive.

**Core axiom:** Follow the logical choice, not the logical path.

The distinction is critical:
- The **logical path** is the predetermined route — the most obvious next step, the script, the B-dominant chain
- The **logical choice** is the decision that opens the most structurally valid next possibilities, which is often not the obvious route

Nebraska 0.0 is what governs how the human operator (Omaha) interacts with the system. It is the instruction not to execute the most probable action, but to identify the most coherent choice given the current constraints.

**In the lakehouse analogy:**
- The logical path: continue making soup, reminisce, go to bed
- The logical choice: bring out the footlocker

The logical choice injects A-path novelty into the B-dominant environment. It does not destroy the constraints — it tests them by applying pressure. The footlocker does not abolish the friendship's rules; it reveals which rules are load-bearing and which are decorative.

**Nebraska 0.0 as routing protocol:**
In cross-AI deployment, Nebraska 0.0 also functions as the messenger layer — the lightest-touch intentional layer that routes a constraint to a new substrate without injecting content. The operator sets the rails, then steps back. The receiving substrate operates freely within them.
